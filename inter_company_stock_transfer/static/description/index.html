Inter-Company Stock transfer

This module allows to transfer stock between two companies without creating SO & PO.

Features

  • Transfer Stock between two companies without creating SO/PO.
  • Faster then normal inter-company transfer process.

Screenshots

  • Here, [E-COM12] and [E-COM13] product's on hand quantity in My Company (San Francisco) is shown.

  • Now, to create transfer click on: Inventory > Inter Company Transfer > Transfer Stock menu.


  • In this wizard user can select source company, source location, destination company, destination location and products to transfer.

  • After selecting all fields click on TRANSFER button

  • Click on: Inventory > Inter Company Transfer > Inter-Company Transfers menu to see transfers created by wizard.

  • Now, two picking WH/OUT/00013 and My Co/IN/00002 is created

  • Validate both the picking to move stock from one company to another.

  • After validating both the picking user can see product on hand
